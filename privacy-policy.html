Introduction

TunisGO app is a transit navigation app for Tunisia's transportation network, developed independently. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

We have designed TunisGO with a privacy-first philosophy: the app works fully offline, stores all transit data locally, and does not track your behavior or location.

2

Data we collect

Location data ✗ Not collected
Analytics / usage tracking ✗ Not collected
Device identifiers ✗ Not collected by us
Account email & name Optional — only if you register
Subscription status Managed by RevenueCat
Favorite routes / preferences ✓ Stored locally only

All transit schedules, routes, and your saved preferences are stored exclusively on your device. We never send this data to our servers.

3

Optional account

TunisGO can be used completely without an account. If you choose to register, we collect only your email address and display name to:

— Sync your preferences across devices
— Manage your subscription (Pro tier)
— Send you important service updates

This data is stored securely on our backend (InsForge) and is never sold or shared with third parties, except as described in section 4.

4

Third-party services

Displays ads on the free tier. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data. You can opt out via your device's ad settings.
Google Privacy Policy →

💳

RevenueCat

Manages in-app purchases and subscription status. RevenueCat receives purchase transaction data from the app store — we do not process payment data directly.
RevenueCat Privacy Policy →

🗄️

InsForge (Backend)

Stores optional account data (email, name) and hosts transit database updates (OTA). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

5

Children's privacy

TunisGO is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your account data at any time. You can:

— Delete your account directly from the app Settings → Account
— Request a full data export by emailing us
— Opt out of personalized ads via device settings

Account deletion removes all your data from our servers within 30 days.

7

Updates to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notification. The effective date at the top of this page always reflects the latest version.
